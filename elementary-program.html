// about the program

What is this program?

A 4-week online STEM adventure for K–5 students — 4 fun sessions per week on Zoom. Each session is a hands-on activity covering science, technology, engineering, coding, and more.

Run entirely by student volunteers through MaxSTEM. No materials needed — everything happens live online. Just show up, have fun, and learn something new every day.

// who can join

Who is it for?

Any K–5 student who wants to explore STEM in a fun, low-pressure environment. No experience needed — just curiosity and a device with Zoom.

Spots are capped at 20 students to keep sessions interactive and personal. Parents and guardians are welcome to observe any session.
