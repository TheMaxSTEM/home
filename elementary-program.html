// about the program

What is this program?

Our Online Elementary School STEM Program is a free homework help and enrichment program built for K–5 students who want extra support in science, technology, engineering, math — and beyond.

Run entirely by student volunteers through MaxSTEM, this program connects young learners with older student mentors who can help explain concepts, work through problems, and make learning fun.

// who can join

Who is it for?

Any elementary school student in grades K–5 who needs help with schoolwork, wants to explore STEM topics, or just needs a friendly face to help them understand a tough concept.

No previous STEM experience required. All skill levels and subjects are welcome. Parents and guardians are encouraged to reach out with any questions.
