// about us

Built by students,
for students.

MaxSTEM was founded by Mohammad Basil, who believes that learning shouldn't stop at the classroom door.I believe every young person deserves access to hands-on STEM experiences — regardless of their background or resources.

We're not a big corporation or a registered nonprofit (yet). We're students who care deeply about education, and we're taking action. Every program, every newspaper issue, every camp — made and run by people just like you.
